Welcome to the bank

To charge your account open a terminal and issue:

curl -X GET http://localhost:9090/Bank/rest/accounts/deposit?customer=Gordon\&amount=10

curl -X GET http://localhost:9090/Bank/rest/accounts/withdraw?customer=Gordon\&amount=10

Or browse these URLs:

"http://localhost:9090/Bank/rest/accounts"

"http://localhost:9090/Bank/rest/accounts/deposit?customer=Gordon&amount=10"

"http://localhost:9090/Bank/rest/accounts/withdraw?customer=Gordon&amount=10"
